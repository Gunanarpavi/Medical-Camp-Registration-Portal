About

About Medical Camp Registration Portal

The Medical Camp Registration Portal is designed to help people easily find free medical camp services, view camp schedules, register for suitable camps, and contact organizers. This portal supports community healthcare outreach by providing camp details, registration support, gallery information, location maps, and an AI help assistant in one simple website.
